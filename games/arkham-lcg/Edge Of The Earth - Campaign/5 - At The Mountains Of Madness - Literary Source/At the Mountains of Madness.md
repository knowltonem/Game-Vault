# At the Mountains of Madness

> *H.P. Lovecraft (1936)*
> *Public domain — originally published in Astounding Stories, February–April 1936*

The source material that connects Outpost 31 → Edge of the Earth. Lovecraft's Antarctic novella introduces the Elder Things, the shoggoths, and the cyclopean city beneath the ice.

## Connection to this campaign

- **Elder Things** → the alien race beneath the ice in Edge of the Earth
- **Shoggoths** → the organism in Outpost 31 is essentially a rogue shoggoth — a shape-shifting biological weapon that escaped the city
- **The City** → "City of the Elder Things" scenario in Edge of the Earth
- **Lake's discovery** → echoes the ice core samples from Outpost 31's Dr. Voss
- **Dyer's warning** → "It's not the cold we should be afraid of"
