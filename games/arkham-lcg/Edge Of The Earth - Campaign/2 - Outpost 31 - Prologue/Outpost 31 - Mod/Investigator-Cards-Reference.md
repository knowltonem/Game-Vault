## Full Roster

### R.J. MacReady — Guardian
*Helicopter Pilot*
**Traits:** *Drifter. Veteran.*

| WP | INT | COM | AGI | HP | SAN |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 4 | 3 | 5 | 2 | 9 | 6 |

**Signature Ability — "I Know What I Am"**
[Action] Exhaust R.J. MacReady: **Fight** using any weapon asset you control. If the attacked enemy is an Organism or Monster, you get +3 combat and deal +1 damage for this attack. (Limit once per round.)

**Elder Sign:** +2 combat. If fighting an Organism or Monster enemy, deal 1 additional damage.

**Front flavor:** *"I know I'm human. And if you were all these things, then you'd just attack me right now."*

**Deckbuilding:** Guardian 0–5. Survivor 0–2. Signature: "I Know What I Am". 1 Weakness + 1 Basic Weakness.

**Signature Ability — "I'm In Command Here"**

### Dr. Copper — Mystic
*Camp Physician*

| WP | INT | COM | AGI | HP | SAN |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 4 | 4 | 1 | 2 | 6 | 9 |

**Signature Ability — "I Can Still Save Him"**
[Action] Exhaust Copper and spend 1 supply: Heal 2 damage or 2 horror from any investigator or Crew Member at your location. If the target has 3 or more suspicion, heal 1 additional damage or horror.
