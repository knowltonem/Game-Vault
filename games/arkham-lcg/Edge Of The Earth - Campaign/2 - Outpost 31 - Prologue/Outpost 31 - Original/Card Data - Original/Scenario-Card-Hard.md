# Scenario Card B — Outpost 31 (Hard / Expert)

## Card Identity
- **Type:** Scenario Card (Chaos Token Reference)
- **Side:** B (Hard / Expert)
- **Set:** Outpost 31
- **Set Icons (bottom right):** Snowflake 1b, Snowflake 1P
- **Icon:** Snowflake (❄)
- **Difficulty:** HARD / EXPERT

## Chaos Token Effects

| Token | Effect |
|---|---|
| Skull (red) | -X. Where X is the highest suspicion on a Crew Member (max 3). |
| Cultist (green) | -3. If you fail, either place 1 Suspicion on your investigator or on a Crew Member at your location. |
| Tablet (blue) | -3. Lose 2 resources. If you cannot, place 1 suspicion on your investigator. |
| Elder Thing (white) | -4. If this skill test is during a **Repair** action and you fail, place 1 suspicion on the scenario card and 1 damage on that location. |

## Differences from Easy/Standard (Card A)

| Token | Easy/Standard | Hard/Expert |
|---|---|---|
| Cultist | -2, fail effect | -3, same fail effect |
| Tablet | -3, fail: lose 2 resources | -3, always lose 2 resources + suspicion if you can't |
| Elder Thing | -4, suspicion on scenario card | -4, suspicion on scenario card AND 1 damage on location |

## Notes
- Skull token identical between difficulties
- Hard/Expert Elder Thing adds location damage — more punishing on Repair failures
- **Repair** is bolded on card
- Card A has typo "loose" — Card B correctly says "Lose"
