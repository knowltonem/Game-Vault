## Organism (Crushing Tendrils)

### Card Identity
- **Type:** Enemy (Organism mode)
- **Card Numbers:** 22b (paired with Elliot Childs 22a)
- **Set:** Outpost 31
- **Set Number:** SSP (bottom right)
- **Illustrator:** Roland MacDonald
- **Icon:** Snowflake (❄)

### Stats
- **Fight:** 3 / **Health:** 4 / **Evade:** 3

### Traits
*Monster. Infected.*

### Keywords
Hunter.

### Game Text
**Forced –** When you reveal an [cultist] token while fighting or evading Organism: Take 1 horror.
**Forced –** After you deal damage to Organism by an effect other than an attack: Draw the top card of the encounter deck.

### Mod Status
⚪ SKIP — Period-neutral.

### Notes
- Cultist token trigger: take 1 horror (vs Creeping Chaos elder thing → damage)
- Non-attack damage (e.g. abilities) forces encounter card draw — punishes clever play

## Notes (All Organism Modes)
- Each Organism mode enemy is paired with a specific Crew Member (same card number, a/b)
- When a Crew Member is revealed as Infected, their card flips to the Organism mode enemy
- All share Monster. Infected. traits and Hunter keyword
- Back face is the story asset card back (art only, no text)
