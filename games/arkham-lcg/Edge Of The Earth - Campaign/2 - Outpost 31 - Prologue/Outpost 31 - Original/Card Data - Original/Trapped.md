# Trapped A/B — Card Data (Original)

## Card Identity
- **Type:** Treachery
- **Card Numbers:** A=66, B=67
- **Set:** Outpost 31
- **Set Numbers:** A=6/7 (ee), B=7/7 (e7) — final two cards of Organism encounter set
- **Illustrator:** Roland Macdonald
- **Icon:** Organism encounter icon (circular tentacle symbol)
- **Subtype:** Treachery

## Traits
*Hazard.*

## Game Text (identical on both copies)

**Revelation –** Test [agility] (3). If you fail and…
- …An Organism enemy is at your location, increase the suspicion level of the nearest Crew Member by 1.
- …An Organism enemy is at another location, it readies, moves (one location at a time) until it reaches your location, and engages you.
- …An Organism enemy is not in play, Trapped gains surge.

## Mod Status
⚪ SKIP — Period-neutral. No changes needed.

## Notes
- Two copies: 66 (6/7), 67 (7/7) — final cards of the 7-card Organism encounter set
- Uses Organism encounter icon, not snowflake
- Three conditional outcomes based on Organism position
- Only treachery that scales with Organism location — elegant design
- "suspicion level" — different phrasing from "suspicion" used elsewhere. Means the same thing.
