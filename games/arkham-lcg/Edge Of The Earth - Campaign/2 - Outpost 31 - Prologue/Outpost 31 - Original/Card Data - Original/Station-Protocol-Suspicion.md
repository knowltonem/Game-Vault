# Station Protocol: Suspicion — Card Data (Original)

## Card Identity
- **Type:** Story Asset (Special)
- **Card Number:** 30
- **Set:** Outpost 31
- **Subtype:** Story (shown at bottom of card)
- **Icon:** Snowflake (❄)
- **Cost:** None (in play from scenario setup)

## Game Text

While a Crew Member has 1 or more suspicion:
↔ That asset cannot be readied by player card effects.

While a Crew Member has 2 or more suspicion:
↔ Increase the cost of the Crew Member's ability by 1 resource/supply/secret.

When a Crew Member has 3 or more suspicion:
↔ Place 1 suspicion on the scenario card.

---

If an investigator has 3 or more suspicion:
↔ Place 1 suspicion on the scenario card.
↔ That investigator cannot Parley.
↔ Other investigators cannot commit cards to their skill tests.

If an investigator has 4 or more suspicion:
↔ That investigator discards 1 card from their hand.

---

Any investigator with Suspicion on them may perform the following action:

[Fast icon]: Test [willpower] (3). If you succeed, remove 1 suspicion from your investigator. (Limit once per round.)
