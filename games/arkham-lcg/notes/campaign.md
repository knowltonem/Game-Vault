# Campaign Mode, XP & Trauma

← [[games/arkham-lcg/index|Back to Arkham LCG]]

---

Campaign play is the default, intended way to play Arkham. Standalone scenarios exist, but the game is built around linked campaigns where your investigators grow and scar.

## Experience (XP)
Earned each scenario (plus **Victory X** cards). Spent **between scenarios** to upgrade level-0 cards to higher-level versions; cost = card level. Your deck visibly improves across the story.

## Trauma
| Type | Cause | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| **Physical** | Defeated by damage (health → 0) | -1 max health, carried forward |
| **Mental** | Defeated by horror (sanity → 0) | -1 max sanity, carried forward |

Enough trauma can kill or drive an investigator insane — permanently removed.

## The Campaign Log
A shared record of XP, trauma, story choices/resolutions, cards gained/lost, and chaos-bag changes.

> Tip: keep the log digitally — one note per campaign under `games/arkham-lcg/campaigns/<name>/log.md`.

## Chaos bag across a campaign
Contents change by campaign and difficulty (Easy / Standard / Hard / Expert); scenarios add/remove tokens and set symbol-token effects.
