## Scenario-by-scenario substitutions

### Curtain Call

| Original (Old Core) | FFG Suggests | DerBK Suggests |
|---|---|---|
| Striking Fear | Torment | **Hallucination** |
| Rats | Whippoorwills / Bystanders | **Arcane Lock** |
| — | — | **Bystanders** (still) |

> **Why:** Hallucination + Arcane Lock gives you a taste of the "locked in" tension that Striking Fear's Frozen in Fear provides in the theater's final stretch. Whippoorwills don't fit well here — Bystanders serve thematically (guests, stage hands).

---

### The Last King

| Original | FFG Suggests | DerBK Suggests |
|---|---|---|
| Ancient Evils | Cosmic Evils | **Cosmic Evils** |

Straight swap, no notes.

---

### Echoes of the Past

| Original | FFG Suggests | DerBK Suggests |
|---|---|---|
| Midnight Masks Treacheries | Dead Ends | **Dead Ends** |
| Dark Cult | Cultists | **Cultists** |
| Locked Doors | Arcane Lock | **Arcane Lock** |

> **⚠️ Important:** Treat enemies from the Cultists set as if they had the same "Spawn – Any empty location" text that the original Dark Cult has. The scenario heavily factors this into how enemies appear across the map.

---

### The Unspeakable Oath

| Original | FFG Suggests | DerBK Suggests |
|---|---|---|
| Agents of Hastur | Hallucinations | **Flying Terrors** |

> **Why:** Hallucinations has no enemy for the Monster pile. Flying Terrors gives you the Bat Horror as a solid stand-in for a Byakhee.
>
> **Setup:** Bat Horrors start in the Monster pile (one of seven cards there).

---

### Phantom of Truth

| Original | FFG Suggests | DerBK Suggests |
|---|---|---|
| Agents of Hastur | Hallucinations | **Flying Terrors** |
| Midnight Masks Treacheries | Dead Ends | **Eldritch Lore** |

> **⚠️ Important:** The ***Byakhee*** trait is mechanically relevant here. Pretend the Bat Horror has the Byakhee trait.

---

### The Pallid Mask

| Original | FFG Suggests | DerBK Suggests |
|---|---|---|
| Ghouls | Gangs of Arkham, Ashen Pilgrims | **Cult of the Yellow Sign** (from Carcosa campaign sets) |
| Chilling Cold | Bad Weather | **Reeking Decay** |

> **Why:** FFG's suggestion is a thematic miss (rain, gangsters, fire cultists in the catacombs). Reeking Decay fits the catacombs thematically and mechanically replaces Chilling Cold well. Cult of the Yellow Sign replaces Ghouls while preserving the Humanoid→Corpse Dweller mechanics without clashing themes.

---

### Black Stars Rise

| Original | FFG Suggests | DerBK Suggests |
|---|---|---|
| Ancient Evils | Cosmic Evils | **Cosmic Evils** |
| Dark Cult | Cultists | **Cultists** |

> **Note:** Surge doesn't stack. If scenario text gives Surge to Ancient Evils, it only adds to the first mode on Cosmic Evils (the second mode already surges — you don't draw an extra card). You end up with two choices, both drawing a new encounter. This is fine.

---

### Dim Carcosa

| Original | FFG Suggests | DerBK Suggests |
|---|---|---|
| Agents of Hastur | Hallucinations | **2x Eager for Death** (from Whippoorwills set — treacheries only, not the full set) |
| Striking Fear | Torment | **Torment** |

> **Why:** Horror is a huge deal in this scenario. Eager for Death is the closest approximation to Rotting Remains. He doesn't use Flying Terrors here — there are already plenty of big monsters. He splits the Whippoorwill set to avoid bloating the deck.

## General notes

- Treat the **Bat Horror** (from Flying Terrors) as a Byakhee when the Byakhee trait matters
- The Cultists enemies need "Spawn – Any empty location" in Echoes of the Past — don't forget it
