# The Five Classes (Factions)

← [[games/arkham-lcg/index|Back to Arkham LCG]]

---

Every player card belongs to one of five classes (plus **Neutral**). Each investigator builds mainly from one or two. These are Arkham's equivalent of LOTR's spheres.

| Class | Color | Identity | Good at |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Guardian** | Blue | Protector / fighter | Combat, soaking damage, defending |
| **Seeker** | Yellow | Investigator / scholar | Clues, card draw, knowledge |
| **Rogue** | Green | Opportunist | Resources, evasion, high-risk/reward |
| **Mystic** | Purple | Spellcaster | Willpower, spells, chaos-bag tricks |
| **Survivor** | Red | Underdog | Recursion, fail-forward, scrappy economy |
| **Neutral** | Grey | Universal | Cards any investigator can take |

## Picking a first class

| If you want to... | Try a... |
|---|---|
| Punch monsters and protect friends | **Guardian** |
| Solve the mystery fast | **Seeker** |
| Play slippery and money-rich | **Rogue** |
| Cast spells and bend luck | **Mystic** |
| Scrape by and recur your best cards | **Survivor** |
