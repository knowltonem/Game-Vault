# Tips & Strategy

← [[games/arkham-lcg/index|Back to Arkham LCG]]

---

## General
- **Clues win games** — make sure someone can investigate reliably.
- **The agenda is a clock** — doom accrues every Mythos phase; don't dawdle.
- **Action economy is everything** — only 3 actions per turn.
- **You don't have to kill everything** — evading is often better than fighting.

## Deckbuilding
- Build to your investigator's strengths and access.
- Mind the curve; include **economy** and **card draw**.
- Respect your **weaknesses**; spend XP fixing your deck's weak point.

## In play
- **Mulligan aggressively** for key early pieces.
- Don't over-commit skill cards to tests you'll already win.
- Leave a margin on critical tests — the **auto-fail** can ruin a "safe" test.

## Multiplayer
- More investigators = more encounter cards but more actions.
- Split **enemy management** and **clue gathering** deliberately (Seeker + Guardian is the classic backbone).
