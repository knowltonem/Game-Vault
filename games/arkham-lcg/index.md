# Arkham Horror: The Card Game (LCG)

> A cooperative, campaign-driven Living Card Game for 1-4 players. Take on the role of an investigator unraveling Lovecraftian mysteries across linked scenarios. Your choices carry forward — investigators gain experience and trauma, and the story branches based on success and failure. No PvP — survive together or fall together.

**Publisher:** Fantasy Flight Games / Asmodee
**My starting point:** the **Core Set (2026)** — the "Chapter Two" relaunch (1-4 players in one box) that replaced the 2021 Revised Core Set as the evergreen base.
