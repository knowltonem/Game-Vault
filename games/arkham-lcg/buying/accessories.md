## 1. Sleeves — essential

You handle these cards constantly across campaigns. Sleeve day one.

| Option | Price | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Gamegenic Art Sleeves (official) | $8.99 / 50pk | Investigator or Encounter back designs |
| Dragon Shield Matte | ~$12 / 100 | Industry standard, very durable |
| Ultra Pro Eclipse | ~$10 / 100 | Good shuffle feel, matte finish |

The 2026 Core has **357 cards**. Budget **500 sleeves** to cover everything with spares.

Detailed recommendation → [[games/arkham-lcg/buying/sleeves|Sleeves]]

## 2. Chaos tokens — nice upgrade

The cardboard tokens work, but you draw from the bag constantly — acrylic feels much better.

| Option | Price | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Gamegenic Premium Chaos Tokens + Bag | ~$30-40 | Official, 44 acrylic tokens + bag |
| BuyTheSameToken (Etsy) | ~$20-30 | Wide variety of designs |
| Burger Tokens | ~$15 | Affordable, good quality |

## 4. Storage — wait until you grow

| Option | Price | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Game Night Chest (Gamegenic) | $119.99 | Premium all-in-one. **Caveats:** rulebooks don't fit, only 4 deck tomes (Core = 5 investigators). Skip unless going all-in. |
| Investigator Deck Tomes | $24.99 each | Class-colored deck boxes. Nice-to-have. Fit in Game Night Chest. |
| BCW shoebox / generic | ~$5-10 | Works fine, much cheaper |
| Broken Token organizer | $89 | Fits everything if you go deep |

## 5. Free: Arkham Cards App

Replaces the chaos bag, campaign log, and token tracking on your phone. **Best value at $0.**

→ https://arkhamcards.com

---

## Priority buy order

1. **Sleeves** (non-negotiable)
2. **Arkham Cards app** (free)
3. **Acrylic chaos tokens** (if you want the tactile upgrade)
4. **Playmat** (if table space is tight)
5. **Storage** (wait until you have more than the Core)
