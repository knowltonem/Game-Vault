## Recommended purchase order

### 1. Core Set (2026) — "Chapter Two"
**Your starting point and mandatory first purchase.** SKU **AHC100**, MSRP **$69.99**, released March 20, 2026. Plays **1–4 players out of one box**, includes the 3-scenario *Brethren of Ash* introductory campaign, 5 new investigators with pre-built decks, the full token/chaos-bag set, plus encounter dividers and connection tokens. Replaced the 2021 Revised Core Set as the evergreen base. See [[games/arkham-lcg/collection/core-set-2026|the full note]].

### 2. A full campaign (Investigator + Campaign pair)
Modern campaigns are sold as two boxes — an **Investigator Expansion** (player cards) and a **Campaign Expansion** (scenarios/encounter cards). You need **both** to play that campaign; buy them together.

### 3. Chapter Two content
The 2026 Core Set begins **Chapter Two**; future scenarios target this newer, smaller pool (older sets become the "legacy" pool — still playable). Watch for: **Children of Blood** (small campaign, summer 2026), the **Arkham Grimoire** accessory, and new **Investigator decks**.

> [!tip] Starting fresh with the 2026 Core means you can ignore the legacy back-catalog at first and just follow Chapter Two.

## Smart buying tips
- Always check for discounts off MSRP.
- Buy Investigator + Campaign pairs together — half a campaign isn't playable.
- Browse sets on **ArkhamDB** before buying.
- Sleeve everything (see [[games/arkham-lcg/buying/sleeves|sleeves]]).
- See [[games/arkham-lcg/buying/accessories|accessories]] for tokens, playmats, storage, and the free Arkham Cards app.

## Legacy campaigns for Chapter 2 owners
If you're starting from the 2026 Core, see [[games/arkham-lcg/notes/legacy-content|the legacy buying guide]] for which old campaigns work best and how to handle encounter set substitution.

The first legacy campaign I'd recommend is [[games/arkham-lcg/notes/carcosa-guide|The Path to Carcosa]] — best story, forgiving with a shallow card pool, and fully playable with DerBK's substitution table.
