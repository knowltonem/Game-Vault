## Card size
Arkham uses **standard card size (63.5 × 88 mm)** — same as most LCGs. Any "standard"/"card game" sized sleeves fit.

## Recommendation
- **Dragon Shield Matte (standard)** — durable, opaque backs, shuffles well.
- Opaque backs matter: encounter and player cards must be indistinguishable from the back.

## How many
Total your owned cards (track in [[games/arkham-lcg/collection/my-collection|my collection]]) and buy 100-packs with a little spare.

> [!tip] Arkham is a heavy-handling campaign game — sleeve from day one.
