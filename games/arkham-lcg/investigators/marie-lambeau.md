## Background
Jazz singer with a gift for the occult. Marie gets an extra action when she successfully casts a spell — making her one of the most action-efficient Mystics in the game. Built around spell cycling.

## Deckbuilding
- Mystic cards level 0–5
- Survivor cards level 0–2

## Notes
- Extra action on successful spell cast — very powerful
- High sanity — can take horror well
- Spell-cycling engine — rewards building around spells
- Strong in solo or as the team's Mystic
