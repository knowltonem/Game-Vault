## Background
Jazz singer and witch. Marie draws on her Grand-Mère's dark legacy of blood magic. After damage is placed on her, she draws 2 cards and discards 1 of them (Limit once per phase), turning pain into card advantage.

## Deckbuilding
- Mystic cards level 0–5
- Neutral cards level 0–5

## Signature
- **Grand-Mère's Charm** — Asset (Accessory. Item. Charm. Occult. Blessed). 2 resources. Uses 3 charges. Action: spend 1 charge: heal 1 damage from an investigator at your location or add 1 charge to a spell you control.

## Weakness
- **Called to Guinée** — Treachery (Curse. Pact.). Revelation: put into play in Marie's threat area. While attached, Marie cannot heal damage. Action: discard 2 random cards from your hand to discard Called to Guinée.

## Notes
- Damage → card draw engine (Limit once per phase)
- High willpower + intellect — flexible spellcaster
- Charm-based signature — supports spell-heavy builds
- Weakness is punishing — requires careful health management
