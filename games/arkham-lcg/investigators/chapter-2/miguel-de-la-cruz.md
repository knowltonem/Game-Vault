## Background
Argentine rancher and tracker. Miguel can take an additional action each turn, which may only be used to play an event. His signature knapsack provides tools for the hunt, and his weakness stalks him relentlessly.

## Deckbuilding
- Survivor cards level 0–5
- Neutral cards level 0–5

## Signature
- **Miguel's Knapsack** — Asset (Body. Item. Supply). 2 resources. Uses 3 supplies. Action: spend 1 supply: search your deck for a Trap or Trick card and add it to your hand. If that card has a cost of 2 or less, you may play it immediately at no cost.

## Weakness
- **Feline Hybrid** — Enemy (Creature. Mutated.). 1 fight, 3 health, hunter. Spawn engaged with Miguel. Prey: Miguel only. When Feline Hybrid is defeated, shuffle it back into the encounter deck instead of discarding it.

## Notes
- Extra action for events — unique action economy
- Signature knapsack finds Trap/Trick cards
- Weakness is recurring — must be dealt with repeatedly
- Dual 8 health/sanity — very balanced durability
