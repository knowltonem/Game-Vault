## Background
Former spy. Evasion-focused Rogue — she gathers clues and slips past enemies rather than fighting them. High intellect makes her a capable investigator as well.

## Deckbuilding
- Rogue cards level 0–5
- Seeker cards level 0–2

## Signature
- **Covert Ops** — Event. 0 resources. Fast. Evade a non-Elite enemy at your location with a bonus equal to Trish's intellect. If you succeed, automatically discover 1 clue at your location.

## Weakness
- **Black Chamber Operative** — Treachery. Revelation: put into play engaged with Trish a Black Chamber Operative enemy (3 fight, 2 health, hunter, 1 damage on spawn). Trish cannot parley with or evade this enemy.

## Notes
- Evasion-focused — avoids rather than kills
- High intellect + agility makes her versatile
- Good for solo or small group play
