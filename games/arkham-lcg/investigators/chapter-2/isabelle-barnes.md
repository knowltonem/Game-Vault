## Background
Jenny Barnes's sister. Wields twin .45s. Survivor class with a Mystic lean — she's scrappy and resourceful but has an otherworldly edge. One of the five new investigators introduced in Chapter Two.

## Deckbuilding
- Survivor cards level 0–5
- Mystic cards level 0–2

## Signature
- **Isabelle's Twin .45s** — Unique asset (Firearm). +1 combat, deals +1 damage. Uses 3 ammo. When Isabelle defeats an enemy, you may search your deck for a copy of Isabelle's Twin .45s and put it into play (Limit once per round.)

## Weakness
- **Breaking Point** — Revelation: take 1 horror for each clue on your location (max 5). If Isabelle has 5 or more horror, shuffle Breaking Point back into your deck instead of discarding it.

## Notes
- Strong agility — good at evading
- Lower combat than her guns might suggest — relies on card effects
- One of the most popular of the Chapter Two five for new players
