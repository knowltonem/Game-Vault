## Background
Jenny Barnes's sister. Wields twin .45s. Survivor class with a Mystic lean — she's scrappy and resourceful but has an otherworldly edge. One of the five new investigators introduced in Chapter Two.

## Deckbuilding
- Survivor cards level 0–5
- Mystic cards level 0–2

## Notes
- Strong agility — good at evading
- Lower combat than her guns might suggest — relies on card effects
- One of the most popular of the Chapter Two five for new players
