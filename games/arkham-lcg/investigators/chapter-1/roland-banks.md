## Background
FBI agent whose recent cases have led him to conclusions the public isn't ready for. A solid all-rounder — strong combat but with enough intellect to gather clues on the side. One of the most accessible investigators in the game for new players.

## Signature
- **Asset:** .38 Special — fires extra when defeating enemies
- **Weakness:** Cover Up — must clear covered-up clues or take mental trauma

## Deckbuilding
- Guardian cards level 0–5
- Seeker cards level 0–2

## Notes
- Discovers a clue at his location after defeating an enemy
- Hybrid fighter/investigator — good solo and in groups
- Often considered the reference point against which other investigators are measured
