## Background
Former prize fighter who puts his fists to better use defending Arkham. Fights with events rather than weapon assets — a unique Guardian archetype that plays very differently from Roland Banks.

## Signature
- **Asset:** Brass Knuckles — boosts combat and enables fighting bare-handed
- **Weakness:** Indebted — ongoing resource drain

## Deckbuilding
- Guardian cards level 0–5
- Neutral cards only (no secondary class)

## Notes
- Fights with events — "Fight" event cards instead of weapon assets
- Very focused archetype — single-minded but effective
- Low intellect and agility — needs support
- One of the most recommended Chapter 1 starter decks
