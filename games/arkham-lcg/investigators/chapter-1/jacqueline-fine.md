## Background
Psychic who sees the future in her dreams. More chaos token manipulation than almost any other investigator — she can cancel, ignore, or replace tokens, making the chaos bag work in her favour.

## Signature
- **Asset:** Jacqueline's Deck of Fate — manipulates chaos tokens drawn
- **Weakness:** Hypnotic Therapy — recurring horror each round

## Deckbuilding
- Mystic cards level 0–5
- Neutral cards only (no secondary class)

## Notes
- Highest sanity in the Chapter 1 starters
- Chaos token manipulation — treats bad draws as opportunities
- Very low combat and agility — needs protection
- Recommended for new players getting into Mystic
