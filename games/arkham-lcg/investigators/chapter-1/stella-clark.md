## Background
Rural mail carrier who's seen things on her route no one would believe. Embodies the Survivor "fail forward" archetype — when she fails a skill test, she gains an extra action. Failure becomes a resource.

## Signature
- **Asset:** Neither Rain nor Snow — free move and free action under certain conditions
- **Weakness:** Wronged — a treachery that triggers from failed tests

## Deckbuilding
- Survivor cards level 0–5
- Neutral cards only (no secondary class)

## Notes
- Extra action on failing tests — turns bad luck into advantage
- Balanced stats — no major weakness
- Considered the most broadly powerful of the Chapter 1 starter five
- Excellent for new players and veterans alike
