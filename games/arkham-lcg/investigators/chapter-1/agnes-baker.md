## Background
Waitress who has begun to discover memories of a past life as a powerful sorceress. When she suffers horror, she deals 1 damage to any enemy at her location — making her pain a weapon.

## Signature
- **Asset:** Heirloom of Hyperborea — draws cards and generates resources
- **Weakness:** Dark Memory — a dangerous spell that damages and adds doom

## Deckbuilding
- Mystic cards level 0–5
- Survivor cards level 0–2

## Notes
- Highest willpower in the core set — exceptional spell caster
- Horror-trigger ability makes her a high-risk, high-reward investigator
- Survivor secondary gives unusual utility options
- Strong in experienced hands
