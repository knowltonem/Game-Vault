## Background
Ex-con troubled by the mad ravings of his former cellmate. His ability lets him spend 2 resources to gain an extra action — potent but expensive, requiring strong resource generation to fuel.

## Signature
- **Asset:** On the Lam — evade all enemies at your location for one round
- **Weakness:** Searched for Weapons — a treachery that damages and limits options
