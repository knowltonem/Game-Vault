## Background
Professor and occult scholar. His ability triggers whenever any investigator draws cards — sharing his knowledge widely. Built around the "Big Hand" archetype — keeping a large hand of cards and rewarding you for doing so.

## Signature
- **Asset:** Vault of Knowledge — increases hand size and has a card draw reaction
- **Weakness:** The Experiment — a persistent monster that follows him

## Deckbuilding
- Seeker cards level 0–5
- Neutral cards only (no secondary class)

## Notes
- Card draw engine — draws more cards whenever cards are drawn
- High hand size — can hold large amounts of cards
- Terrible agility and combat — extremely fragile
- Surprisingly tough at 7 health for a Seeker
