## Background
Librarian with custody over books that perhaps never should have been written. Highest base intellect of the core set — exceptional clue-gatherer. Her Tome ability gives her an additional action to use Tome cards each turn.

## Signature
- **Asset:** Daisy's Tote Bag — increases hand size and has a useful reaction
- **Weakness:** The Necronomicon — a cursed tome that forces discard or horror

## Deckbuilding
- Seeker cards level 0–5
- Mystic cards level 0–2

## Notes
- Gets a free action to use Tome cards — powerful with the right build
- Considered one of the best investigators in the full card pool
- Frail in combat — needs teammates or protection assets
