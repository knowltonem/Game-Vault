## Background
Daring aviator known as the "woman without fear." When she commits two or more cards to a skill test, she draws a card — making her a high-velocity card-cycling machine built around oversucceeding tests.

## Signature
- **Asset:** Borrowed Time — gain 3 actions immediately, but take 2 horror later
- **Weakness:** Give in to Despair — a treachery that deals horror when cards are committed

## Deckbuilding
- Rogue cards level 0–5
- Neutral cards only (no secondary class)

## Notes
- Oversucceeding focus — rewards committing many skill icons
- Fast deck cycling — burns through cards quickly
- High agility — excellent evader
- High-risk, high-reward playstyle
