## Background
Street urchin with little to her name besides a mysterious family heirloom. Her ability lets her discard the top card of her deck to cancel a chaos token draw and redraw — a powerful safety net in tough situations.

## Signature
- **Asset:** Wendy's Amulet — cancel the first chaos token drawn each round
- **Weakness:** Abandoned and Alone — reshuffles discard pile into deck and takes horror

## Deckbuilding
- Survivor cards level 0–5
- Rogue cards level 0–2

## Notes
- Chaos token manipulation — very flexible ability
- High agility — excellent evader
- Very low combat — never fights if she can help it
- Strong solo investigator
