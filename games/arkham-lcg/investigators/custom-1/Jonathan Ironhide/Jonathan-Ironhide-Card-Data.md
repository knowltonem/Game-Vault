# Jonathan Ironhide — Custom Guardian

**Pairing:** Designed to run with Alistair Greystoke (Seeker). Ironhide handles enemies; Greystoke handles clues.

---

## Story

Jonathan Ironhide should be dead.

By any reasonable measure he died in 1923, somewhere in the South Pacific, when the vessel he was crewing was pulled beneath the surface by something vast and cold and impossible. He remembers the descent. He remembers the pressure. He remembers the city.

R'lyeh.

He does not remember how he got back to the surface. He woke on a beach in New Zealand three days later with no memory of swimming, no explanation for his survival, and a scar across his face that hadn't been there before. The scar didn't come from the wreck. He knows that. He doesn't know what made it.

What he does know is that he saw the sunken city. He walked its impossible geometry. He stood in the presence of something so ancient and so wrong that it rewired something fundamental in his mind. He came back changed — harder, colder, and permanently marked by R'lyeh's geometry.

He didn't leave empty handed either. The Hollow Warden came back with him — he found it in his coat when he woke on that beach, though he had no memory of picking it up. The Hydra Hyde was wrapped around him, dry despite the ocean. Gifts from the Deep, or curses. He stopped trying to decide which.

The nightmares never stopped. Every time he defeats an enemy, every time he pulls the trigger, he hears the city. Feels it. The horror of R'lyeh doesn't leave a man. It chips away at him, kill by kill, until there's nothing left but the killing.

He found Alistair Greystoke through mutual contacts in Arkham's underground network of Mythos hunters. Greystoke had been tracking the same threads for decades. Ironhide didn't trust the old man at first — didn't trust anyone who seemed too comfortable with the Mythos. But Greystoke was right about enough things often enough that Ironhide stopped arguing and started shooting.

They don't talk much. They don't need to. Greystoke finds the clues. Ironhide clears the room. It works.

*"I've seen the sunken city. The rest of you just read about it."*

## Design Notes

- **Core loop:** Defeat enemies → gain resources → fund expensive gear → defeat more enemies. Self-sustaining combat engine.
- **Weakness tension:** Echoes of R'lyeh punishes his ability directly — every kill that generates a resource also costs 1 horror. His sanity of 6 means this pressure is real and accumulates fast.
- **Two signature requirements:** Hollow Warden + Hydra Hyde are always available from setup. Consistent opener every game.
- **Hollow Warden:** Strong gun with card draw on non-Elite kills. Keeps hand fueled while dealing damage. Subtitle "Unholy" and R'lyeh flavor tie directly to his origin story.
- **Hydra Hyde:** Simple always-on damage reduction with 3/3 soak. Keeps Ironhide in the fight longer. The second flavor line — "I didn't leave R'lyeh empty handed" — ties both signature items to the same origin moment.
- **Deckbuilding splashes:**
  - Mystic 0–2: Ward of Protection (1), Read the Signs (1), Holy Water (1)
  - Survivor 0–1: Lucky! (0), A Test of Will (1), Take Heart (0), Drawing Thin (1)
- **Stat profile:** 5 Combat is top tier for Guardians. 9 Health is very high — built to absorb punishment. 6 Sanity is the pressure valve — the weakness erodes it kill by kill.
- **Pairing:** Designed to stand between threats and Greystoke. High health, damage reduction, and reactive ally damage make him extremely durable in the front line.
