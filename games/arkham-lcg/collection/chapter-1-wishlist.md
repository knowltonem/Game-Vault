## Deal Evaluation Formula

When you find a lot:

```
Lot price ÷ (new MSRP of everything included) = % of MSRP
```

- **Under 40%** — excellent deal, buy immediately
- **40–55%** — good deal, normal secondhand range
- **55–70%** — fair, acceptable if condition is excellent or includes hard-to-find OOP items
- **70%+** — not worth it unless sealed/mint or includes rare promos

## Where to Look (ranked by likely success)

1. **BoardGameGeek GeekMarket** — boardgamegeek.com/geekmarket — best for complete organized collections from serious hobbyists
2. **r/boardgameexchange** and **r/arkhamhorrorlcg** (Reddit) — active trade threads, no listing fees so often cheaper
3. **eBay** — search "arkham horror lcg complete cycle" or "arkham horror lcg lot" sorted by price+shipping lowest first
4. **Facebook Marketplace** + Arkham Horror LCG Buy/Sell/Trade Facebook groups — high local activity, no shipping cost if local pickup
5. **Local FLGS used game sections** — sometimes trade-ins surface complete sets cheap

## Notes
- Revised format boxes (Campaign Expansion + Investigator Expansion) are sufficient on their own — they reprint everything from the original Mythos packs. You don't need both old and revised format of the same cycle.
- Watch for "old format" sellers bundling original Mythos packs — these are increasingly OOP and can command higher prices than the revised reprints despite being functionally identical content.
- Always confirm card counts in the listing photos match expected totals above before buying.
