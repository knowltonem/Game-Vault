## Overview

Hit-and-run cavalry deck where characters dash in, trigger effects, and intentionally leave play. More dynamic than the Dwarf deck — constantly cycling allies in and out to chain abilities and maintain momentum.

**Spheres:** Spirit + Tactics | **Starting threat:** 28

| Hero | Sphere | Threat | Role |
|---|---|---|---|
| Eomer | Tactics | 10 | Gains +2 Attack when any character leaves play |
| Lothiriel | Spirit | 9 | Puts a Rohan ally into play free during questing — leaves at end of phase, triggering Eomer |
| Hirgon | Tactics | 9 | Engage an enemy and bank a free Tactics resource when you do |

---

## Key mechanics

**Lothiriel combo** — quest phase: she drops a Rohan ally into play free to quest. End of phase: ally leaves, Eomer gets +2 Attack, leaves-play effects fire, ally returns to hand. Free willpower + combat burst every round.

**Leaves play triggers** — Eomer, Eomund, Westfold Horse-breeder all have abilities that fire when a Rohan ally leaves. This is intentional, not a drawback.

**Feint + Forth Eorlingas!** — Feint cancels an attack; Forth Eorlingas! lets Rohan characters attack during the quest phase. Together they give huge tempo swings.

---

## Tips

- Always keep 2-3 Rohan allies in hand at quest phase start for Lothiriel
- Firefoot on Eomer lets him splash damage to a second enemy — devastating with his attack burst
- Hirgon rewards aggression — pull enemies to you on your terms, not theirs
- A Test of Will is in this deck — always prioritize cancelling nasty Treacheries
- Eowyn from the core set slots in perfectly — high willpower fits the cavalry questing style
