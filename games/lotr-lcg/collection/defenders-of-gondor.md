## Overview

Gondor swarm deck built around flooding the board with Gondor allies and triggering powerful synergies. Focuses on strength in numbers — cheap allies that get dramatically better when fielded together. More aggressive and straightforward than the Dwarf deck.

**Spheres:** Leadership + Tactics | **Starting threat:** 29

## Key mechanics

**Gondor synergy** — most cards get stronger with more Gondor characters in play. Build a wide board fast.

**Boromir's ready ability** — pay 1 threat to ready him after attacking or defending. Lets him act multiple times per round at a cost. Manage carefully.

**Denethor's scry** — look at the top encounter card every round and optionally move it to the bottom. Lets you dodge nasty enemies and treacheries with planning.

**Sentinel + Ranged** — Tactics access gives Gondor defenders and ranged attackers that can assist across decks in multiplayer.

---

## Tips

- Steward of Gondor on Boromir gives him massive resource generation
- Sneak Attack + Gandalf works great here too for threat reduction
- Denethor's scry is most powerful — always use it even if you don't move the card
- Pairs well with Spirit heroes for threat management since Boromir's ability raises threat
- Keep Boromir's readying in reserve for clutch combat moments, not every round
