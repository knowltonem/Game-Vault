## Key player cards to know

**Steward of Gondor** (Leadership) — +2 resources per round. Best economy card in the game.
**A Test of Will** (Spirit) — Cancels any encounter card. Essential in almost every deck.
**Unexpected Courage** (Spirit) — Readies a hero so they can act twice per round.
**Gandalf** (Neutral) — Draw 3, deal 4 damage, or reduce threat by 5. Leaves play at end of round.
**Feint** (Tactics) — Cancels an enemy attack entirely for the round.
**Sneak Attack** (Leadership) — Plays an ally for 1 resource, returns to hand. Combo with Gandalf.
