## Overview

Combo/swarm deck built around flooding the board with Dwarf allies and triggering powerful bonuses once you have 5+ Dwarves in play. Delves aggressively through the deck — drawing cards fast, playing allies cheaply, snowballing hard.

**Spheres:** Leadership + Lore | **Starting threat:** 26

| Dain Ironfoot | Leadership | 11 | All Dwarves get +1 Attack and +1 Defense while he's ready |

---

## Key mechanics

**5 Dwarf threshold** — many cards get dramatically better once you have 5+ Dwarves in play. Build toward this every game.

**Delving** — cards let you look at the top of your deck and pull Dwarves directly into play, bypassing resource costs.

**Ori draw chain** — every Dwarf ally played draws a card. With Legacy of Durin attached, chain draws through the whole planning phase.

**Dain's passive** — keep him ready at all costs. His buff applies to every Dwarf without him doing anything else.

---

## Tips

- Open with We Are Not Idle and Lure of Moria to flood the board fast
- Put Steward of Gondor on Dain — he needs the most resources
- Resist committing Dain to the quest; his passive is worth more
- Sneak Attack + Gandalf works great for recurring threat reduction
- Pairs well with Spirit heroes from the core set for threat control
