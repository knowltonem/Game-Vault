## Deck A — The Three Hunters

**Aragorn** (Leadership) — Threat 12. Quests then readies to defend or attack same round. Steward of Gondor on him gives 3 resources per round — fuel for everything.

**Legolas** (Tactics) — Threat 9. Every kill places 2 progress tokens on the quest. Combat IS questing. Equip weapons early.

**Gimli** (Tactics) — Threat 11. Gets +1 Attack per damage on him. Let him take hits deliberately. With Dwarven Axe and 3 damage on him he attacks for 6+.

### Key cards

| Card | Sphere | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Steward of Gondor | Leadership | On Aragorn — 3 resources per round, priority one |
| Unexpected Courage | Spirit | On Aragorn — quest AND defend/attack same round |
| Dwarven Axe | Tactics | On Gimli — boosts attack |
| Feint | Tactics | Cancel an enemy attack entirely |
| Quick Strike | Tactics | Attack before enemy swings |
| Sneak Attack + Gandalf | Leadership | 1 resource for -5 threat — use when climbing |
| Rivendell Blade | Tactics | On Legolas — reduces enemy defense for easier kills |

### How to play

**Early (rounds 1-3):** Get Steward of Gondor on Aragorn first. Let Gimli take a hit or two. Use Feint to protect heroes while setting up.

**Mid (rounds 4-7):** Equip Legolas with weapons — each kill now drops 2 progress tokens. You win the quest through combat. Sneak Attack Gandalf when threat hits 40+.

**Late:** Gimli with 4 damage + Dwarven Axe = 6+ attack, one-shots most enemies. Aragorn with Unexpected Courage quests, readies, defends, attacks — does everything in one round.

## Deck B — The Support Deck

**Eowyn** (Spirit) — Threat 9. Does all the questing Deck A cannot. Base 4 willpower. Discard cards from hand to boost willpower by 1 each — use to push through tough quest stages.

**Theodred** (Leadership) — Threat 8. Passes 1 resource to another hero every round when he quests. Always send it to Aragorn in Deck A — doubles Deck A's economy.

**Ori** (Lore) — Threat 8. Lore access for healing and card draw. Draws a card every time you play a Dwarf ally. Keeps both hands full.

### Key cards

| Card | Sphere | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| A Test of Will | Spirit | Cancel any encounter card — always hold one in hand |
| The Galadhrim's Greeting | Spirit | Reduce each player's threat by 3 — safety valve |
| Unexpected Courage | Spirit | Second copy on Theodred — quest and pass resource same turn |
| Hasty Stroke | Spirit | Cancel a shadow card effect |
| Dunedain Warning | Leadership | +1 Defense on Aragorn or Gimli |

| Either player hits 45 | Sneak Attack Gandalf -5 threat |
| Either player hits 48 | Direct play Gandalf emergency |
