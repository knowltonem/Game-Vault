## ENCOUNTER DECK — PASSAGE THROUGH MIRKWOOD

### Step 1 — Pull these three encounter sets
- Passage Through Mirkwood (scenario-specific cards)
- Spiders of Mirkwood (spider enemies + web treacheries)
- Wilderland (forest locations + treacheries)

Set all other encounter cards aside — do not mix in.

### Step 2 — Pull quest cards out first
Find cards 1A, 1B, 2A, 2B, 3A, 3B — stack in order with 1A on TOP face down. These do NOT go in the encounter deck.

### Step 3 — Shuffle everything else
All remaining enemies + locations + treacheries shuffled into one face-down deck.

### Step 4 — Read quest card 1A
Flip 1A face up. Place **Old Forest Road** (location) face up in staging area. Follow any other setup instructions on the card.

## Priority Turn 1

1. Play Steward of Gondor on Aragorn — non-negotiable
2. Theodred always quests and passes resource to Aragorn
3. Hold A Test of Will in Deck B hand at all times
4. Let Gimli take the first hit — charges his attack bonus
