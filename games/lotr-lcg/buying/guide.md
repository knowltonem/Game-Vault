## Recommended purchase order

### 1. Revised Core Set (~$70 MSRP)
**Mandatory first purchase.** Everything needed for 1–4 players. 3 scenarios + campaign mode. Look for 10–20% discounts — rarely need to pay full price.

- [Asmodee store](https://store.asmodee.com/products/lotr-lcg-revised-core-set)
- [FFG product page](https://www.fantasyflightgames.com/en/products/the-lord-of-the-rings-the-card-game/products/the-lord-of-the-rings-the-card-game-revised-core-set/)
- [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=lotr+lcg+revised+core+set)
- [Miniature Market](https://www.miniaturemarket.com/ffgmec101.html)

---

### 2. The Dark of Mirkwood (~$20)
2 intro scenarios that continue the Revised Core Set campaign. Good second purchase — no new player cards, but adds quest content and campaign continuation before jumping into full cycles.

---

### 3. Starter Decks — *optional, read first*
Four themed decks: Defenders of Gondor, Dwarves of Durin, Elves of Lórien, Riders of Rohan (~$22 each).

> [!caution] Community consensus: **skip the starter decks** if you plan to buy full cycle expansions. They contain overlapping cards with the cycles, you won't get 3 copies of everything, and you'll end up with duplicates. They're useful if you want a second pre-built deck to play 2-player with a friend before investing in expansions.

---

### 4. Cycle expansions (Hero + Campaign box)
Each cycle comes in two revised boxes:
- **Hero Expansion** — all the new player cards for that cycle
- **Campaign Expansion** — all the quest/encounter cards + campaign content

You need **both** to get the full cycle. Buy them together.

| Cycle | Theme | Status |
|---|---|---|
| Angmar Awakened | Angmar / undead north | ✅ Available |
| Dream-chaser | Sea, sailing, corsairs | ✅ Available |
| Ered Mithrin | Grey Mountains, dragons | ✅ Available (2024) |

---

### 5. Saga expansions — *endgame content*
Book-based scenarios covering the events of The Lord of the Rings. 3 revised boxes (one per book volume), each containing 6 scenarios. **Best experienced after you have strong decks** — difficulty is higher than cycle content.

| Box | Status | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Fellowship of the Ring Saga | ✅ Available | |
| The Two Towers Saga | ✅ Available | |
| The Return of the King Saga | ✅ Available (Oct 2024) | Combines Flame of the West + Mountain of Fire |

> [!note] **The Hobbit Saga has NOT been revised.** If you want those scenarios, buy the original Hobbit Saga boxes (Over Hill and Under Hill, On the Doorstep).

---

## What about old adventure packs?

The old "Shadows of Mirkwood" cycle (the first cycle, 6 adventure packs) has not received a revised repackaging. Individual adventure packs are still compatible but may be harder to find. The community expects a revised repackaging at some point — if you're patient, it may be worth waiting.

---

## Smart buying tips

- Always search for discounts — 10–20% off MSRP is consistently findable
- Miniature Market and Amazon are reliable sources
- For out-of-print older packs, check CoolStuffInc, local game stores, and BGG marketplace
- RingsDB lets you browse every card before buying — useful to see what's in a set
- Don't buy the digital/online version — widely considered poor value
