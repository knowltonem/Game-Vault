## Card Size

Every card in LOTR LCG — player cards, encounter cards, quest cards, heroes — is **63.5 × 88mm** (standard card game size). Same size as Arkham Horror LCG, Marvel Champions, most FFG LCGs. One size fits all.

## Two-Color Strategy

If buying individual packs, split by card type:

| Color | Card type | Approx count |
|---|---|---|
| Color 1 (e.g. Black) | Player cards — heroes, allies, attachments, events | ~350 |
| Color 2 (e.g. Green) | Encounter cards — enemies, locations, treacheries | ~290 |

At a glance during setup and cleanup you instantly know which pile is which. Saves time every session.

## Notes

- Always buy a few extra packs — sleeves tear during sleeving occasionally
- Do NOT use the 66 × 91mm size — that's for different games, too loose for LOTR LCG
- Perfect Fit inner sleeves (64 × 89mm) can be used under Dragon Shield for double-sleeving if you want maximum protection, but single sleeve is fine for home play
