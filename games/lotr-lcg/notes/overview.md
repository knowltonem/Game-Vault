## What is an LCG?

A **Living Card Game** is a fixed-distribution card game — unlike a CCG (like Magic), every copy of a pack contains the exact same cards. No randomized booster packs, no card rarity chase, no secondary market for singles. If you want a card, buy the pack it's in and you're guaranteed to get it.

---

## The core loop

Each scenario gives you a **quest deck** (your objective) and an **encounter deck** (the game's threats). Each round you try to make progress on the quest while the encounter deck throws enemies, treacheries, and locations at you. Run out of time (threat hits 50) or lose a hero and it's over.

---

## What's in your deck

You build a **player deck** of ~50 cards before the game and choose **3 hero cards** separately (heroes start in play, not in your deck).

| Card type | What it does |
|---|---|
| Heroes | Your characters. Start in play. Generate resources each round. |
| Allies | Additional characters played from hand using resources. |
| Attachments | Equipment/upgrades that stay on a hero or ally. |
| Events | One-shot effects played from hand, then discarded. |

---

## Revised vs. original — what actually changed

The rules and cards are **identical**. "Revised" updated the box contents only:

| What changed | Original | Revised |
|---|---|---|
| Copies of player cards | 1–2 of some | 3 of every card |
| Threat trackers | 2 | 4 (one per player) |
| Token denominations | 1 only | 1, 3, and 5 |
| Campaign mode | ❌ | ✅ (new campaign/boon/burden cards) |
| Player count support | Needed 2 boxes for 4p | 4p out of the box |

All old expansions, adventure packs, and saga boxes are **fully compatible** with the Revised Core Set.

---

## The encounter deck (how the game "plays itself")

You don't control the encounter deck — it's the game's AI. Each Quest phase you flip over cards equal to the number of players:

- **Enemy cards** — sit in the staging area adding their Threat value, then engage and attack when the time comes
- **Location cards** — also add Threat to staging, and block quest progress until explored
- **Treachery cards** — immediate nasty effects, then discarded
