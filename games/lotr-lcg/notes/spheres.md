# The Four Spheres

← [[games/lotr-lcg/index|Back to LOTR LCG]]

Each card in the game belongs to one of four **spheres** (resource types). Your heroes generate resources in their own sphere. To play a card, you must spend resources from a hero whose sphere matches the card.

Multi-sphere decks are possible but require careful hero selection.

---

## Leadership *(blue)*

**Flavour:** Strength of command, mustering armies, coordination.

**Strengths:**
- Resource generation and acceleration
- Synergy and buffing other players' characters
- Strong allies with high hit points
- "Secrecy" and cost-reduction mechanics

**Best for:** Supporting other players, multiplayer, resource-hungry decks.

**Example cards:** Steward of Gondor (massive resource gen), Faramir (willpower buff), Denethor.

---

## Lore *(green)*

**Flavour:** Knowledge, nature, healing, preparation.

**Strengths:**
- Card draw (the best in the game)
- Healing damage from heroes and allies
- Card advantage and deck manipulation
- Strong in solo for sustained card flow

**Best for:** Solo play, long campaigns, support roles.

**Example cards:** Gleowine (card draw), Lórien's Wealth, Daughter of the Nimrodel (healer).

---

## Tactics *(red)*

**Flavour:** Direct combat, battle, martial power.

**Strengths:**
- Highest attack and defense values
- Direct damage abilities
- Cancelling shadow effects
- Ranged and sentinel keywords

**Best for:** Killing enemies fast, handling heavy combat scenarios.

**Example cards:** Legolas (progress on kills), Gimli (berserk mode), Feint (cancel attack).

---

## Spirit *(purple)*

**Flavour:** Hope, courage, speed, resilience.

**Strengths:**
- Threat reduction (crucial for staying below 50)
- Highest willpower for questing
- Event cancellation
- Location control

**Best for:** Solo threat management, fast questing, avoiding enemies.

**Example cards:** Éowyn (massive questing), A Test of Will (cancel any encounter card), The Galadhrim's Greeting (threat reduction).

---

## Mixing spheres

You can include cards from any sphere in your deck — but you can only *pay* for them with resources from a hero of the matching sphere. Most decks use 2 spheres for consistency.

> [!tip] The most beginner-friendly combo is **Spirit + Lore**: great card draw, strong questing, and threat control. Add Leadership or Tactics once you know the game better.
