## Win & loss conditions

**Win:** Place enough progress tokens on the final quest card to complete all quest stages.

**Lose (any of these):**
- Any hero dies (damage ≥ hit points)
- Your collective threat reaches **50**
- You must draw a card but your deck is empty

---

## The 7 phases of each round

### Phase 1 — Resource *(simultaneous)*
Each hero gains 1 resource token on their card. Each player draws 1 card.

### Phase 2 — Planning *(in turn order)*
Spend resources to play **allies**, **attachments**, and **events** from your hand. Resources must match the sphere of the card being played (or a matching hero must control them).

### Phase 3 — Quest
1. Each player optionally **commits characters** (exhaust them) to the quest — they contribute their Willpower.
2. Reveal encounter cards equal to the number of players from the encounter deck — add to the staging area.
3. Total **Willpower committed** vs. total **Threat in staging area**:
   - Willpower > Threat → place the difference as **progress tokens** on the active quest card
   - Threat > Willpower → raise each player's **threat** by the difference

### Phase 4 — Travel *(optional)*
Move **one location** from the staging area to become the **active location**. Its threat no longer counts in the staging area. Progress tokens go to the location first before reaching the quest card.

### Phase 5 — Encounter
- Each player may optionally **engage** one enemy from the staging area (bring it to their area).
- Any enemy whose **engagement cost** is ≤ your current threat **automatically engages** you.

### Phase 6 — Combat
1. **Deal shadow cards** — each engaged enemy gets one face-down card from the encounter deck.
2. **Enemies attack** (in turn order) — each player assigns a defender (exhaust them). Undefended attacks deal damage directly to heroes. Flip the shadow card for a bonus effect.
3. **Players attack** — declare attackers (exhaust them). Combined Attack vs. enemy Defense; excess = damage on the enemy. Enemies reaching 0 HP are defeated and discarded.

### Phase 7 — Refresh *(simultaneous)*
- **Ready** (un-exhaust) all exhausted cards.
- Each player raises their **threat by 1**.
- Pass the **First Player token** clockwise.

## Key concepts

**Threat** — your personal "doom clock." Rises every round. When it hits 50 you lose. High threat means more enemies auto-engage you.

**Exhausting** — rotate a card 90° to show it's been used this round. Cards ready at the Refresh phase.

**Willpower** — a character stat used to make quest progress. Questing is the core way to win.

**Staging area** — the shared zone where revealed enemies and locations sit. Their printed Threat values add up against your questing each round.

**Shadow cards** — dealt face-down to attacking enemies. Flipped when that enemy resolves its attack — can trigger nasty bonus effects.

**Engagement cost** — the threat value at which an enemy automatically drags itself into combat with you. Higher = safer to leave in staging.

**Restricted** — attachments with the Restricted keyword: a character can only have 2 Restricted attachments at once.

## Campaign mode

New to the Revised Core Set. Chain scenarios together as a linked narrative:
- **Boon cards** — permanent bonuses earned by completing scenarios well
- **Burden cards** — ongoing penalties for failures or story choices
- Heroes can carry injuries or upgrades between scenarios
- Campaign starts with the 3 Revised Core Set scenarios, continues into expansion products that include campaign cards (Dark of Mirkwood, Angmar Awakened, etc.)
